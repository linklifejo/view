    1) swiper.js : The Most Modern Mobile Touch Slider 
    2) slick.js  : the last carousel you'll ever need - Ken Wheeler
    3) bxslider.js : Add a slider to any webpage.
    4) 그밖에 ...검색어) jquery slide, jquery carousel, free carousel..

  ※ 유료 모델은 License 비용을 지불 ==> $ [환율...]
